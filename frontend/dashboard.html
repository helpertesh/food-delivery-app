Enter the number that receives M-Pesa prompts. You will get a Lipa na M-Pesa popup — enter your PIN to pay.

Manual pay still available: send to 0712919229 if STK is unavailable — use Pay with M-Pesa after paying so we can record your order.

📱 How STK push works

  1. Tap Pay with M-Pesa — Safaricom Daraja sends a payment request to your phone.
  2. When the prompt appears, confirm the amount and enter your M-Pesa PIN.
  3. When payment succeeds, your order is placed automatically.
